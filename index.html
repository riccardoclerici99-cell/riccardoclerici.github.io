ABOUT ME

Adaptive music
for every medium

Game & Film Composer based in Milan, Italy. Bachelor's in Composition from CPM Music Institute (2025), with a thesis on the dramatic and psychological role of horror music in cinema and video games.

I specialize in adaptive audio, interactive scoring, and end-to-end production: composition, recording, mixing, and mastering. I work across video games, film, fashion, and theatre.

Winner of the Campiello Giovani Award 2022 for the sound design of "La Spartenza".
