ABOUT ME

Adaptive music
for every medium

Game & Film Composer based in Milan, Italy. Bachelor's in Composition from CPM Music Institute (2025), with a thesis on the dramatic and psychological role of horror music in cinema and video games.

I specialize in adaptive audio, interactive scoring, and end-to-end production — composition, recording, mixing, and mastering. I work across video games, film, fashion, theatre, and pop music, collaborating with directors and game designers to align musical language with visual storytelling.

Winner of the Campiello Giovani Award 2022 for the sound design of the nationally recognized short story "La Spartenza".
